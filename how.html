How does COVID-19 spread?



COVID-19 spreads mainly by droplets produced as a result of coughing or sneezing of a COVID-19 infected person.This can happen in two ways:




  • Direct contact: One can get the infection by being in close contact with COVID-19 patients


  • Indirect Contact:The droplets survive on surfaces and clothes for many days.Therefore, touching any such infected surfaces or cloth and then touching one's mouth,nose or eyes can transmit the disease
