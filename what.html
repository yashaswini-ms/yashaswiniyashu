What is this COVID-19?



COVID-19 is a disease caused by the “novel corona virus”.
Common symptoms are:
  • Fever
  • Dry cough
  • Breathing difficulty

    • Some patients also have
  • aches and pains
  • nasal congestion
  • runny nose
  • sore throat or diarrhoea
